Research Intrestes

My research is in the field of operations research and mathematical optimization, specifically on Mixed-Integer Linear Programs. The primary focus of my work is on developing solution algorithms and mathematical models to better capture the complexities in the supply chain operations and logistics, mainly in the area of vehicle routing. I'm also very interested in Quantum programming and the application of OR techniques in the field of Machine Learning (Check my course projects section).

Research Projects:

  • ML-assisted Vehicle Routing Under Uncertainty
  • Developing Branch-Price-and-Cut Solver for Periodic Vehicle Routing Problems
  • Last-mile delivery policy optimization based simulation framework
